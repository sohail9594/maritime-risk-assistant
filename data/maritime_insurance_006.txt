DATE: June 22, 2026
SOURCE: Heavy Marine Transport Underwriters
SUBJECT: Liability Caps on Out-of-Gauge (OOG) Cargo
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Insurance Premium Update
DETAILS: Due to an unprecedented number of collapsed flat-rack containers and gantry crane accidents, the maximum insurance payout for standard Out-of-Gauge (OOG) cargo (machinery exceeding standard container dimensions) has been capped at $2 Million USD per unit. Shippers moving heavy industrial equipment valued over $2 Million must purchase supplemental "Project Cargo Coverage," which increases the base shipping freight cost by an additional $15,000 USD per unit.
