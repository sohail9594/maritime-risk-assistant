SUBJECT: Military Rations (MREs) with Flameless Heaters
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Dangerous Goods Classification
DETAILS: Logistics planners frequently misclassify Meals, Ready-to-Eat (MREs) as standard food/humanitarian aid. However, MREs containing Flameless Ration Heaters utilize powdered magnesium and iron. When exposed to high maritime humidity or water, they emit flammable hydrogen gas. Therefore, pallets of self-heating MREs are legally classified as Class 4.3 Dangerous Goods (Dangerous When Wet) and cannot be stored in unventilated holds.
