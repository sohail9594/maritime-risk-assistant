DATE: June 12, 2026
SOURCE: UN Framework Convention on Climate Change
SUBJECT: Atmospheric Water Generator (AWG) Impoundment
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Environmental Law Enforcement
DETAILS: Atmospheric Water Generators (machines that extract drinking water from desert humidity) are arriving in the Gulf in massive numbers. However, customs agents are actively enforcing the Kigali Amendment. Any AWG utilizing highly potent greenhouse gases (specifically HFC-134a or R-410A refrigerants) is legally banned from entering the GCC. Only units certified to use ultra-low GWP refrigerants (like R-290 or CO2) will clear customs.
