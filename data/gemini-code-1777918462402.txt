DATE: May 27, 2026
SOURCE: GCC Environmental Protection Agency
SUBJECT: Auxiliary Engine Emissions Ban
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Port Regulation
DETAILS: To improve local air quality, all commercial vessels docked at Jebel Ali must utilize "Cold Ironing"—plugging into the port's electrical grid for power instead of running their onboard auxiliary diesel engines. Vessels lacking shore-power plug compatibility are banned from berthing and must remain at offshore anchorages until they secure specialized zero-emission battery barges, adding a minimum of 4 days to standard discharge times.
