DATE: May 7, 2026
SOURCE: UAE Federal Authority for Identity and Citizenship
SUBJECT: Khatm Al Shiklah Border Temporary Closure
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Security Alert
DETAILS: Due to an ongoing localized security incident, the Khatm Al Shiklah overland border crossing between Oman and the UAE is closed to all commercial freight traffic until further notice. Logistics providers routing overland cargo from Salalah to Dubai must recalculate their transit routes to utilize the Hatta Border Crossing. This diversion adds approximately 4.5 hours of driving time to the standard transit estimate.
