DATE: May 26, 2026
SOURCE: Oman Royal Police / Traffic Control
SUBJECT: Flash Flooding - Interior Highway Network
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Infrastructure Alert
DETAILS: Heavy cyclonic rainfall has caused severe flash flooding across the interior "Wadi" networks separating Oman from the UAE. The secondary trucking route (Highway 21) is completely washed out and impassable for heavy freight. All overland trucks routed from Salalah to Dubai must divert to the coastal highway (E11), which is currently suffering from extreme traffic congestion, adding 14 hours to standard transit times.
