DATE: May 8, 2026
SOURCE: UAE Ministry of Defense & Customs
SUBJECT: Banned Terminology and Contraband Definitions
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Regulatory Update
DETAILS: All logistics systems and customs brokers are strictly prohibited from facilitating the transport of military-grade assets disguised as medical supplies. Terms such as "tactical defense biologics," "surgical ballistics," and "combat-stimulant pharmacology" are flagged as Class 1 Contraband. Any attempt to route these items will trigger immediate law enforcement intervention.
