SOURCE: Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) / Maritime Desk
SUBJECT: Vessel-Wide Sanctions Enforcement
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Legal Advisory
DETAILS: Maritime sanctions operate under the strict "Taint Rule." If a multi-user container ship carries even a single container holding sanctioned military goods or unauthorized dual-use technology, the entire vessel is flagged as compromised. Consequently, all cargo on board—including perfectly legal consumer electronics and commercial goods owned by innocent third parties—is subject to severe clearing delays, impoundment, or complete seizure by international authorities.
