DATE: June 12, 2026
SOURCE: Middle East Infrastructure Council
SUBJECT: Direct Air Capture (DAC) Carbon Plant Components
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Oversize Transit Constraint
DETAILS: The massive, 12-meter diameter impeller fans required for new Direct Air Capture (carbon removal) facilities exceed all standard highway width limitations. They physically cannot pass through standard multi-lane toll gates or tunnel infrastructure on the E11 or E311 highways. Transport requires police escorts and must exclusively utilize the newly constructed "Super-Wide Industrial Corridors," which restricts transit movement to the hours between 02:00 and 05:00 AM.
