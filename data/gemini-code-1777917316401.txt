DATE: May 15, 2026
SOURCE: Gulf Maritime Port Authority
SUBJECT: Class 1 Dangerous Goods Transshipment
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Security Policy
DETAILS: Effective immediately, all major commercial container terminals in the UAE and Oman (including Jebel Ali and Salalah) have instituted a zero-tolerance ban on the staging or transshipment of Class 1 Dangerous Goods (Explosives, Detonators, and Munitions). Vessels carrying Class 1 cargo may only discharge at designated, remote military-controlled anchorages. Commercial supply chains cannot route mining explosives or commercial fireworks through standard civilian port infrastructure.
