DATE: May 11, 2026
SOURCE: Regional Automotive Freight Council
SUBJECT: Roll-on/Roll-off (RoRo) Discharge Capabilities
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Port Capabilities Update
DETAILS: Automotive shipments arriving on RoRo vessels cannot be diverted to standard container feeder ports or bulk terminals. Discharging vehicles requires specialized stern-ramp infrastructure and massive, secure tarmac staging yards (minimum 50,000 sqm free space). In Oman, only the primary terminals at Salalah and Sohar possess the necessary RoRo infrastructure to handle deep-sea car carrier diversions.
