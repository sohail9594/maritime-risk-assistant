DATE: May 11, 2026
SOURCE: Middle East Petrochemical Alliance
SUBJECT: Shelf-Life of Hydrocracking Catalysts
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Material Handling Alert
DETAILS: Shipments of highly reactive industrial hydrocracking catalysts are currently en route to GCC refineries. These chemical agents have a strict marine-humidity exposure limit. If transit and staging delays exceed 21 days from the date of factory sealing, the catalysts begin to degrade and turn inert, rendering them useless for refining operations. Shipments approaching the 21-day limit must be expedited via airfreight to prevent total cargo failure.
