DATE: May 4, 2026
SOURCE: Gulf Meteorological Center
SUBJECT: Flash Flooding on E311 Transit Corridor
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Weather Alert
DETAILS: Severe unseasonal storms have caused localized flash flooding on the primary overland trucking routes connecting Salalah (Oman) to the UAE border. The Royal Oman Police have temporarily restricted heavy freight movement. All overland transit from Salalah to Dubai is currently experiencing a mandatory 48-hour weather delay. Conditions are expected to clear by May 6.
