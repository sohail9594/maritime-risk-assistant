DATE: May 9, 2026
SOURCE: Gulf Logistics Council
SUBJECT: Repositioning of Empty Cold Chain Assets
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Operational Bulletin
DETAILS: Due to the high volume of medical cargo being offloaded in Oman and trucked to the UAE, there is a severe buildup of empty Cold Chain (Reefer) containers in Sharjah. Sea carriers refusing to enter the Gulf cannot retrieve them. Shippers are now responsible for reverse logistics. Overland trucking of empty Reefer containers from Sharjah back to Salalah incurs a flat repositioning tariff of $800 USD per container.
