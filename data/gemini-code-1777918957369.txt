DATE: May 31, 2026
SOURCE: World Organisation for Animal Health (WOAH)
SUBJECT: H5N1 Outbreak - Total Poultry Transit Ban
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Biosecurity Regulation
DETAILS: Following a confirmed outbreak of highly pathogenic H5N1 Avian Influenza in a major South American export region, the GCC has implemented an absolute border ban on all poultry products originating from or transiting through the affected zone. This ban supersedes all religious, commercial, or humanitarian designations. Even fully certified frozen Halal poultry from this origin will be incinerated at the port of entry without exception.
