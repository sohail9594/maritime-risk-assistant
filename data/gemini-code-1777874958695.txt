DATE: May 1, 2026
SOURCE: Lloyd's Maritime Insurance Syndicate
SUBJECT: War Risk Premium Increase for Arabian Gulf
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Financial Advisory
DETAILS: Due to escalating geopolitical tensions, hull war risk premiums for all commercial vessels transiting the Strait of Hormuz have been raised to a baseline of 5.5% of hull value. Ships carrying high-liability cargo, specifically advanced medical imaging equipment and surgical robotics, face an additional 1.5% surcharge due to total-loss risks. Offloading at the Port of Salalah (Oman) and utilizing overland trucking via the E311 highway is currently exempt from these maritime surcharges.
