DATE: May 12, 2026
SOURCE: GCC Food Security Taskforce
SUBJECT: Berthing Priority for Bulk Staples
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Emergency Directive
DETAILS: In response to regional supply chain instability, the Taskforce has activated Emergency Directive 99A. All GCC port authorities must prioritize the berthing and discharge of bulk carriers transporting foundational staple foods (wheat, rice, barley, and raw sugar). These vessels are granted immediate queue-jumping privileges, superseding the berthing schedules of all containerized consumer goods, electronics, and standard commercial freight.
