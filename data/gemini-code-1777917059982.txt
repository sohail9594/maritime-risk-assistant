DATE: May 10, 2026
SOURCE: Saudi Petro-Logistics Authority
SUBJECT: East-West Pipeline (Petroline) Capacity 
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Alternative Routing Update
DETAILS: For crude oil shipments unable to transit the Strait of Hormuz, the East-West Pipeline (Petroline) offers a viable overland alternative, pumping crude from Eastern Province facilities directly to the Red Sea port of Yanbu. The current transit tariff for utilizing the Petroline is $1.85 USD per barrel. Energy traders must compare this pipeline tariff against the combined cost of maritime War Risk premiums and extended sea-transit fuel costs.
