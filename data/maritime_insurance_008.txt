SUBJECT: Armed Guard Requirements for the Arabian Sea
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Insurance Policy Mandate
DETAILS: Kidnap & Ransom (K&R) premiums for crew and cargo transiting the expanded High-Risk Area (HRA) in the Arabian Sea are fluctuating wildly. To stabilize costs, insurers now mandate the presence of Privately Contracted Armed Security Personnel (PCASP). If a carrier employs a minimum of 3 armed guards on board, the K&R premium remains at the baseline rate. If the vessel transits the HRA without armed guards, the K&R premium instantly surges by 300%.
