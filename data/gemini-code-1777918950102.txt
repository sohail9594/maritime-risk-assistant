DATE: May 29, 2026
SOURCE: NATO Cooperative Cyber Defence Centre
SUBJECT: ECDIS Navigation Ransomware
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Cyber-Physical Threat Alert
DETAILS: A sophisticated ransomware strain is actively targeting the Electronic Chart Display and Information Systems (ECDIS) of ultra-large container vessels. Infected vessels lose all automated navigation and collision-avoidance capabilities. Port authorities in the GCC will not allow any vessel with a compromised ECDIS to enter territorial waters or approach the pilot boarding station due to the risk of grounding. Infected vessels must remain in international waters until a manual cybersecurity hard-reset is performed.
