DATE: May 28, 2026
SOURCE: International Transport Workers' Federation (ITF)
SUBJECT: Coordinated Stevedore Strike Action
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Labor Disruption Alert
DETAILS: In protest of the introduction of fully autonomous AI straddle carriers, the ITF has initiated a wildcat strike affecting all manual stevedore (dockworker) operations at the Port of Salalah and Khalifa Port. Roll-on/Roll-off (RoRo) vessels, Breakbulk, and Out-of-Gauge (OOG) heavy-lift cargoes—which cannot be handled by automated cranes—are permanently stalled. Fully containerized cargo handled by automated STS cranes is unaffected and operating at normal discharge speeds.
