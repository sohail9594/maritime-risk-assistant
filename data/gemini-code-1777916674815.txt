DATE: May 9, 2026
SOURCE: UAE Ministry of Health and Prevention (MOHAP)
SUBJECT: Royal Decree on Critical Care Requisitions
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Legal Advisory
DETAILS: Under Royal Decree 44/2026, the Ministry of Health may issue a "Critical Care Requisition" for any life-saving medical cargo currently delayed at UAE ports. When this decree is activated for a specific shipment, sovereign priority overrides all commercial maritime laws. The cargo is instantly cleared by customs, and all accumulated port tariffs, detention, and demurrage fees are legally nullified and waived.
