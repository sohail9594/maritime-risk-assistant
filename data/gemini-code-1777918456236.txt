DATE: May 25, 2026
SOURCE: World Health Organization / GCC Customs
SUBJECT: Mandatory Vector Control and Fumigation
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Biosecurity Mandate
DETAILS: Due to an outbreak of vector-borne diseases in South Asia, all commercial vessels originating from or transshipping through Mumbai or Colombo must undergo a mandatory 24-hour Methyl Bromide fumigation process before they are permitted to dock at any GCC port. This biosecurity hold applies to the vessel's hull and cargo holds. Cargo cannot be cleared for discharge until the 24-hour fumigation and subsequent ventilation period is complete.
