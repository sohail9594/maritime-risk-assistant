DATE: May 18, 2026
SOURCE: Regional Industrial Safety Board
SUBJECT: Ammonium Nitrate Staging Quotas
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Safety Regulation
DETAILS: Following updated global port safety protocols, no single commercial port facility is legally permitted to store or stage more than 1,000 metric tons of agricultural-grade Ammonium Nitrate at any given time. If a diverted vessel attempts to offload a bulk shipment exceeding this quota at a feeder port like Salalah, the port authority will reject the cargo. Shippers must either split the cargo across multiple vessels or secure direct-to-truck discharge with no port warehousing.
