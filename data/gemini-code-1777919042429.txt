DATE: June 8, 2026
SOURCE: Biological Weapons Convention (BWC) Watchdog
SUBJECT: Gene Editing Tools as Dual-Use Biologics
DOCUMENT_TYPE: International Security Mandate
DETAILS: Industrial-scale CRISPR-Cas9 viral vector kits have been upgraded to Class 1 Dual-Use Biological materials. These shipments require explicit, physical end-user inspections by international health monitors prior to dispatch. Any attempt to route commercial gene-editing kits through "free trade zones" to obscure the final buyer will result in the immediate revocation of the pharmaceutical company's global export license.
