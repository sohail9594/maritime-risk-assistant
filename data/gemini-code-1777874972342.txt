DATE: May 4, 2026
SOURCE: Jeddah Islamic Port Authority
SUBJECT: Trans-Peninsula Transit Times
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Logistics Bulletin
DETAILS: The Port of Jeddah remains a zero-risk discharge alternative for vessels avoiding the Arabian Gulf. However, logistics planners must account for the geographic distance. Overland transit from Jeddah across the Saudi peninsula to Dubai requires specialized long-haul logistics. Current transit time from Jeddah to Dubai by road is exactly 6 days. Medical device distributors must verify battery life and stability for any active medical cargo taking this extended route.
