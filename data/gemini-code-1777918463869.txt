DATE: May 27, 2026
SOURCE: UAE Ministry of Climate Change and Environment
SUBJECT: Desert Locust Swarm Agricultural Threat
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Biosecurity Alert
DETAILS: A massive desert locust swarm is migrating across the Arabian Peninsula. All overland trucks carrying raw agricultural products (grain, fresh produce, livestock feed) arriving from Oman or Saudi Arabia must undergo a rigorous, manual 6-hour biosecurity inspection at the UAE border to ensure no locusts or egg masses are smuggled into the domestic agricultural zone. This delay does not apply to sealed industrial or medical cargo.
