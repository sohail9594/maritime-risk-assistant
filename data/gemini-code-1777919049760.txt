DATE: June 10, 2026
SOURCE: NATO Deep Sea Rescue Command
SUBJECT: SRV Air-Bridge and Port Priority
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Extreme Emergency Maritime Law
DETAILS: In the event of an active submarine distress signal, military Submarine Rescue Vehicles (SRVs) deployed via heavy airlift to the nearest deep-water port assert absolute sovereign override. All commercial port operations, including the offloading of perishable food and critical medical supplies, must instantly halt. Commercial vessels must vacate deep-water berths within 2 hours to accommodate the SRV mothership.
