DATE: June 22, 2026
SOURCE: Freight Forwarders Liability Group
SUBJECT: Demurrage Insurance Voidance for Known Labor Actions
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Policy Voidance Alert
DETAILS: Shippers purchasing "Delay in Transit" or "Demurrage" insurance policies are advised of a new exclusion criteria. Insurers will no longer cover demurrage or detention fees incurred at ports with pre-announced labor union strikes. If a logistics planner routes cargo to a port (e.g., Salalah or Jebel Ali) after a wildcat strike has been officially declared by the ITF, the shipper assumes 100% financial responsibility for all resulting port fees.
