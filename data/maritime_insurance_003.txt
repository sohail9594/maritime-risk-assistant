DATE: June 21, 2026
SOURCE: Global Cargo Underwriters Syndicate
SUBJECT: Premium Increases for Extended Perishable Transits
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Insurance Premium Update
DETAILS: With many carriers diverting around the Cape of Good Hope, maritime transit times from Asia to Europe have increased by an average of 14 days. Consequently, insurance premiums covering "Cargo Spoilage" for Refrigerated Containers (Reefers) containing pharmaceuticals or perishables have been raised by a flat 40%. Policies now also include a strict cap: insurers will not cover spoilage if the total voyage duration exceeds 45 days, regardless of the cause of delay.
