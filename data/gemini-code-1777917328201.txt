DATE: May 17, 2026
SOURCE: GCC Animal Welfare Authority
SUBJECT: Summer Ban on Livestock Imports
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Regulatory Mandate
DETAILS: Due to extreme summer temperatures exceeding 45°C with high humidity, a strict regulatory ban is in place for all sea-borne imports of live sheep and cattle into Gulf ports between June 1st and September 15th. Any livestock carrier arriving during this window will be denied berthing and forced to turn back. Supply chains relying on fresh meat must transition entirely to pre-slaughtered, frozen Halal cargo during this blackout period.
