DATE: June 9, 2026
SOURCE: GCC Halal Food Security Board
SUBJECT: Cultivated Meat and Synthetic Proteins
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Religious and Regulatory Import Ban
DETAILS: Until unified theological and health consensus is reached, the importation of all synthetic, lab-grown, or cultivated animal proteins (including bio-reactor beef and synthetic dairy) is strictly banned across all GCC borders. These products are classified as "Unverified Novel Foods." Any overland cold-chain truck containing lab-grown meat will be turned back at the border, and the importing logistics company will be fined $50,000 USD.
