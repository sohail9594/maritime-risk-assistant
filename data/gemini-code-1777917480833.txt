DATE: May 20, 2026
SOURCE: Global Cargo Underwriters
SUBJECT: End-of-Life / Damaged Lithium Batteries
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Transport Ban
DETAILS: While new Electric Vehicle (EV) batteries can be shipped under standard Class 9 Hazmat rules, "End-of-Life" or physically damaged lithium-ion batteries intended for recycling are completely banned from standard container vessels. Damaged cells are at extreme risk for spontaneous "thermal runaway" fires which cannot be extinguished by water. These must be transported in specialized, blast-proof, liquid-submerged containment vessels.
