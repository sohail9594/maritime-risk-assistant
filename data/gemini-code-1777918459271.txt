DATE: May 26, 2026
SOURCE: Global Semiconductor Fabrication Alliance
SUBJECT: Airborne Particulate Contamination Risk
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Handling Guideline
DETAILS: Unsealed semiconductor wafers and lithography optical components must maintain ISO Class 3 cleanroom integrity during transit. If these shipments are routed overland during a severe dust storm or sandstorm, and the container's micro-filtration seals are compromised, the entire payload is assumed contaminated by silica particulates. Such cargo must be written off as a total loss and cannot enter a fabrication facility.
