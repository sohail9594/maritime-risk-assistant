DATE: May 6, 2026
SOURCE: Emirates SkyCargo Logistics
SUBJECT: Emergency Airlift Charter Rates
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Freight Pricing Update
DETAILS: For medical shipments unable to secure safe maritime or overland transit, emergency air freight charters from Mumbai (BOM) to Dubai Al Maktoum (DWC) are available. Transit time is 4 hours, with a 6-hour customs clearance guarantee for medical goods. However, due to aviation fuel spikes and high demand, air freight currently carries a 1,200% price premium compared to standard maritime routing. This option is recommended exclusively for life-saving biologics or critical shortages where cost is not a factor.
