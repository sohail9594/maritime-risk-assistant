SOURCE: Global Trade Compliance Office
SUBJECT: Port of Bandar Abbas Restriction
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Legal Advisory
DETAILS: Due to updated international trade restrictions, the Port of Bandar Abbas (Iran) is marked as a Level 5 Restricted Zone. Any vessel calling at this port will be subject to secondary sanctions. Logistics providers handling medical supplies and humanitarian aid must route cargo through approved neutral ports (such as Salalah or Jeddah) to avoid immediate seizure of assets and nullification of cargo insurance.
