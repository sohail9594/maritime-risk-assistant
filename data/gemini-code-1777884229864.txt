DATE: May 6, 2026
SOURCE: Red Sea Maritime Authority
SUBJECT: Sudden Capacity Overload at Jeddah Islamic Port
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Operational Update
DETAILS: Due to the massive volume of global carriers diverting away from the Arabian Gulf, the Port of Jeddah is experiencing a sudden, catastrophic capacity overload. All berthing operations are suspended for 48 hours to clear the backlog. The previously established 6-day overland transit time from Jeddah to Dubai is now increased to an estimated 9 to 11 days total due to yard congestion and a severe shortage of long-haul truck drivers.
