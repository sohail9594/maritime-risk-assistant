SUBJECT: Sovereign Gold and Bullion Risk Caps
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Insurance Policy
DETAILS: The overland transportation of sovereign gold reserves, bullion, or physical currency via armored convoy is subject to strict risk-dispersion rules. Insurance syndicates mandate a maximum liability cap of $50 Million USD per individual convoy. If a logistics provider is tasked with moving $200 Million in bullion from a port to a central bank, the shipment must be physically split into four separate convoys, departing at staggered intervals of no less than 4 hours, taking separate geographic routes.
